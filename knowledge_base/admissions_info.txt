=== ADMISSION PROCESS ===

Step 1: Online Registration
- Visit SPPU admission portal: www.unipune.ac.in
- Create student account with email and mobile number
- Receive login credentials

Step 2: Fill Application Form
- Personal details (name, date of birth, address)
- Educational qualifications (10th, 12th marks)
- Category details (caste certificate if applicable)
- Entrance exam details (MHT-CET/JEE Main score)
- Choice of colleges and courses (preference order)

Step 3: Upload Documents
Required Documents:
- Recent passport-size photograph
- Signature scan
- 10th mark sheet and certificate
- 12th mark sheet and certificate
- Leaving certificate/Transfer certificate
- Migration certificate (if from other state board)
- Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC/NT/SBC)
- Caste validity certificate
- Non-creamy layer certificate (for OBC)
- Income certificate (for EWS)
- Domicile certificate (Maharashtra)
- Aadhaar card
- PAN card (if available)
- Gap certificate (if applicable)

Step 4: Pay Application Fee
Application Fees:
- Open Category: ₹800
- Reserved Categories (SC/ST/VJ/NT/SBC): ₹600
- OBC/EWS: ₹700
- Payment Mode: Online (Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI)

Step 5: CAP Rounds (Centralized Admission Process)
- Register for CAP on Maharashtra State CET Cell website
- Fill choice of colleges and courses (up to 100 choices)
- Participate in merit-based seat allocation
- Three CAP rounds conducted
- Accept allocated seat and pay fees

Step 6: Document Verification
- Visit allocated college with original documents
- Physical verification by college authorities
- Submit attested photocopies
- Pay college fees

Step 7: Confirmation of Admission
- Pay tuition fees and other charges
- Collect admission receipt
- Attend orientation program
- Receive student ID card

=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q1: What is the admission process for undergraduate programs?
A: Admission is through merit-based selection or entrance exams like MHT-CET. Apply online, fill CAP form, participate in counselling rounds, and complete document verification.

Q2: Is hostel accommodation available for all students?
A: Hostel accommodation is limited and allocated on merit basis. Priority given to outstation students. Many students opt for private PG/hostels nearby.

Q3: What is the fee structure for different courses?
A: Fees vary by program. Arts/Science/Commerce: ₹12,000-15,000/year. Engineering: ₹77,000-97,000/year. Professional courses: ₹40,000-1,00,000/year.

Q4: Are scholarships available?
A: Yes, multiple scholarships available including government scholarships (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), merit scholarships, and private scholarships. Apply through Mahadbt portal.

Q5: What is the placement scenario?
A: University has active placement cell. Average package: ₹4.5 LPA. Top companies: TCS, Infosys, Wipro, L&T, etc. 70% placement rate.

Q6: Can I pursue distance education?
A: Yes, SPPU offers distance education through Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE) for various UG and PG programs.

Q7: Is there any age limit for admission?
A: No upper age limit for most programs. For engineering, maximum age is 25 years (relaxation for reserved categories).

Q8: How do I apply for caste certificate verification?
A: Submit caste certificate issued by competent authority. University conducts caste validity verification. Certificate must be from Maharashtra state.

Q9: What is the attendance requirement?
A: Minimum 75% attendance required for appearing in university exams. Medical certificates accepted for genuine cases.

Q10: Can I change my subject/branch after admission?
